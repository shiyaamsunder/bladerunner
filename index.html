    Characters

    K

    K, serial number KD6-3.7, later known as Joe was a Nexus-9 replicant Blade Runner.

    Rick Deckard

    Rick Deckard was a Blade Runner of the LAPD who was assigned in 2019 to retire four replicants.

    Joi

    Joi was a brand of DiJi designed by the Wallace Corporation. One of these Joi products was the love interest for Blade Runner K.

    Niander Wallace

    Niander Wallace was a scientist and the founding CEO of Wallace Corporation.

    Luv

    Luv was a Nexus-9 model replicant and the right-hand of Niander Wallace. Luv admired Wallace and his vision for the future of replicants.

    Sapper Morton

    Sapper Morton was a Nexus-8 replicant and combat medic who hid in the outskirts of greater Los Angeles as a protein farmer.

    Prequels

    Blade Runner

    This 1982 film was directed by Ridley Scott and written by Hampton Fancher and David Peoples. It is loosely based on Philip K. Dick's novel Do Androids Dream of Electric Sheep? (1968). The film is set in a dystopian future Los Angeles of 2019, in which synthetic humans known as replicants are bio-engineered by the powerful Tyrell Corporation to work at space colonies.

    Blade Runner Black Out 2022

    Blade Runner Black Out 2022 is a neo-noir cyberpunk anime short film prequel to Blade Runner 2049. While the Replicant NEXUS 6 expired in inventory, TYRELL CORP. pushed the series 8 into the local and Off-world market. The NEXUS series 8 were purpose-built with a natural lifespan. These angry masses used the Replicant Registration database to identify and kill Replicants.

    2036: Nexus Dawn

    2036: Nexus Dawn is a short film acting as a prequel to the feature film Blade Runner 2049 and features Jared Leto as Niander Wallace, alongside Benedict Wong. The film takes place in Los Angeles in 2036, 13 years before the events of Blade Runner 2049 and tells the story of Wallace's meeting with the "lawmakers" and his attempts to convince them to allow his new line of Replicants to enter production.

    2048: Nowhere To Run

    2048: Nowhere To Run is short film acting as a prequel to the feature film Blade Runner 2049 and the sequel to short film 2036: The film takes place in Los Angeles in 2048, one year before the events of Blade Runner 2049 and tells the story of Morton's protecting of a mother and daughter from thugs, ultimately leading to his status as a Replicant being reported.
